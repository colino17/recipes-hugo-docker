side_image: ./images/mongolian-beef.jpg

1. Toss beef strips in corn starch.
2. Combine brown sugar, splenda, garlic, red pepper flakes, ginger, soya sauce, and water in a bowl.
3. Cook beef in skillet with olive oil.
4. Add sauce to the skillet and allow to thicken.
5. Serve with a side of veggies or rice.
